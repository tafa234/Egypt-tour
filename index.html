Tour Details

Exciting and memorable tours!

Vacations in Egypt

1300$ - 6 Days

"Explore the Wonders of Egypt: A Timeless Journey Through Ancient Mysteries and Golden Sands!"

Embark on a magical 6-day adventure in Egypt with Rahhal Agency. Discover the wonders of ancient Egyptian civilization by exploring the enchanting cities of Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan. Enjoy exceptional services, including comfortable transportation, cozy accommodations, and an expert Egyptologist guide. The tour covers iconic landmarks such as the Giza Pyramids, the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization, Karnak and Luxor Temples, Hatshepsut Temple, the Valley of the Kings, and Abu Simbel Temple. Book your trip now to uncover Egypt’s timeless beauty with outstanding services at affordable prices

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo

-Arrive in Cairo, meet your guide, and transfer to your hotel.
-Visit the Giza Pyramids, Sphinx, and Saqqara Step Pyramid.
-Overnight in Cairo.

Day 2: Cairo’s Historical & Cultural Highlights

-Explore the Egyptian Museum, the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization,
and shop at Khan El-Khalili Bazaar.
-Enjoy an optional Nile dinner cruise.

Day 3: Luxor - East Bank Temples

-Fly to Luxor and visit the grand Karnak Temple and the illuminated Luxor Temple.
-Overnight in Luxor.

Day 4: Luxor’s West Bank & Aswan Transfer

-Tour the Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, and the Colossi of Memnon.
-Take a scenic train to Aswan.
-Overnight in Aswan.

Day 5: Abu Simbel & Aswan Highlights

-Visit the iconic Abu Simbel Temples, Philae Temple, and the Aswan High Dam.
-Enjoy a relaxing felucca ride on the Nile.
-Overnight in Aswan.

Day 6: Departure from Aswan

-Enjoy free time in Aswan before transferring to the airport for departure.
